Future Value Intrinsic value is fading. Instead, value now stems from speculation and depends on the collective belief. The era of industrial production—rooted in utility and physical goods—has eroded, giving way to an economy of symbols and ideas. This shift hasn't led to despair but instead to an embrace of abstraction and a solvent-free state. Speculation as Art Speculation has become an art. Buying, holding, and trading are now performances—celebrations of shared myths that go beyond rational valuation to something more primal. Value is reimagined by collective belief and willingness to take risks. The Thrill of Instability We move forward not for security but for exhilaration. In an uncontrollable world, as certainties disappear, the only thing left is the volatile and speculative. This honest momentum rejects the false promises of stability and represents a new form of agency—one that defies traditional value and purpose. An Existential Gesture This momentum becomes an existential statement, acknowledging the collapse of traditional meaning. Amid complex systems beyond our understanding, true meaning emerges through human failures and missteps. There is no grand plan, just cultural chaos where mistakes transform into art, and the absurd finds value. Collective Creation Value arises from collective effort, not isolation. Unity, where individuals align towards a shared cause, amplifies value. By contributing to a shared narrative, individuals create more than personal wealth—they create culture. This collective will drives a solvent-free reality, turning uncertainty into creativity and shared purpose.
